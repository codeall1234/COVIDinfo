Influenza Virus
by Ikshaan Kaul

The influenza virus known as the flu is a contagious respiratory illness. The main cause of the flu is the virus that mutates every year in the winter. The way to catch the flu is to inhale it. The best way to prevent the flu is to get a flu shot every year. This means that getting vaccinated every year to prevent the flu from infecting you. There is no cure for the influenza virus so the best way to prevent it is to get vaccinated. If you catch the flu you should get a lot of rest so your body can recover.
